John MacArthur

John MacArthur is a well-known pastor, author, and teacher.

John MacArthur was the pastor of Grace Community Church in Sun Valley, California, since 1969 until 2025. Was also the president of The Master's University and The Master's Seminary. MacArthur was known for his expository preaching style and his commitment to biblical inerrancy. He has authored numerous books on Christian living, theology, and biblical studies.
